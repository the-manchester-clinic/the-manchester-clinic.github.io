Visit us

By train: Eccles station has hourly services from Manchester Piccadilly, Manchester Airport & Liverpool Lime Street. The station is just a 5 minute walk from The Manchester Clinic.

By tram: With regular trams to much of Greater Manchester, Eccles tram stop is 10 minutes away on foot.

By bus: A number of bus routes run along the nearby Monton Road (5 mins) at a walking distance to the clinic

By car: Situated close to J2 of the M602, The Manchester Clinic has ample free parking and has two charging points for electric cars.
